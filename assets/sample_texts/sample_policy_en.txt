Program: City Health Coverage Assistance
Eligibility: Residents aged 18+ with income below 200% of the federal poverty level. 
Documents required: Proof of identity, residency, and income (last 2 pay stubs or tax return).
How to apply: Complete the online form and upload documents. In-person support available at community centers.
Deadlines: Rolling enrollment; coverage begins the first day of the month after approval.
Notes: Immigration status does not affect eligibility. Assistance available in multiple languages.
